# Pict-DataExplorer

> A config-driven hierarchical "folders" data explorer for Pict applications

- Browse a relational dataset as an expandable tree of records and their child collections
- Expand a Project to see its 5 Users, 10 Media, 3 Documents — each expandable, paginated, drillable
- Three relationship kinds out of the box: one-to-many, many-to-many joins, and belongs-to references
- Resolves lists natively from meadow-endpoints (paginated, Lite column projection, filtered) or any custom source
- Record preview-card popouts on every node, as a soft dependency you can opt into
